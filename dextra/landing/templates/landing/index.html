Create

Express and share your talent and creativity

You may think that your creativity is of little use to the world. But we believe that each drop adds to the beauty of the ocean. Everyone is talented in their own unique way. And no talent should be hidden.

We want to read every poem written on the back of a physics book. We want to see every doodle made on the edge of the crossword. We want to listen to every tune hummed in the rain.

Whatever it is. Dance or music, drama or literature, design or art. Video or audio, image or text. Professional or hobbyist. We have an appetite for all kinds and all levels of talent. So let us bring together the different colors of our right brain. Our Dextra cerebra.

Collaborate

Explore and find all the world's talent and creativity

Every talent that is born and would die, is an abstract resource which adds value to all of humanity. We help use these resources to fill gaps in our expression of the world around us. Gaps which exist in our own talents due to the brevity of our lives. However together we are able to convey our ideas not limited by our individual skill sets, but broadened to the horizons of human expression.

Let not our ideas slip by. Let's make every dream a reality.

You have one life. Together we have a billion.
You may be good at some things. Together we are the best at everything.
